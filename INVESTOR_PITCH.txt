                SECTION 3: HOW IT WORKS (THE SIMPLE VERSION)
================================================================================

Imagine 50 traders sitting in a room, each with a different strategy.
Every day, they all trade. At the end of the month:

    - The top performers get to "teach" the bottom performers.
    - The bottom performers learn new tricks.
    - A few wild cards try completely new approaches.
    - Rinse and repeat for 150 months.

After 150 rounds of this, the surviving strategy has been battle-tested 
against 13 years of every market condition India has thrown at it.

That's our AI.


WHAT IT WATCHES (10 MARKET SIGNALS):
-------------------------------------
Every day, our AI analyses 10 different market signals simultaneously:

    1. TREND STRENGTH    -- Is the market trending or choppy?
    2. MOMENTUM          -- Is the trend accelerating or slowing?
    3. VOLATILITY        -- Is the market calm or agitated?
    4. MEAN REVERSION    -- Has the price stretched too far from average?
    5. SMART MONEY FLOW  -- Are large investors buying or selling?
    6. RISK REGIME       -- Are we in a calm, stressed, or crisis state?
    7. TAIL RISK         -- Is a market crash likely?
    8. STATIONARITY      -- Are patterns reliable right now?
    9. BOLLINGER BANDS   -- Is the price at support or resistance?
    10. VIX (FEAR INDEX) -- How scared is the market?

It processes 20 days of history across all 10 signals = 200 data points,
and makes ONE decision:

    BUY  /  HOLD  /  SELL

That's it. Simple output. Complex brain.
